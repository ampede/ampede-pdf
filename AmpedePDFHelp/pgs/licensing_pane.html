The Licensing Pane

The Licensing pane is where you can register/unregister your copy of PDF. It also provides a handy button to purchase PDF online, and access PDF's feature list.


Purchase

Clicking the Purchase button will open your default web browser and take you to PDF's purchase page. You can buy a license for PDF there.


Info

Clicking the Info button will open your default web browser and take you to PDF's product information page.


Register

After you have purchased a PDF license and received your serial number (via email), you're ready to register PDF. PDF can only be registered with a single license on one machine at a time.

  1. To register your copy, click on the Enter Serial Number button and the LicenseControl application will open.
  2. Enter your serial number in the License ID: text field. You may want to copy and paste from the email you received containing the number.
  3. Click on the Install a License button. After agreeing to the Ampede PDF Software License Agreement (which you should read carefully), LicenseControl will contact the licensing server and register that copy of PDF with your license.
  4. When the License Status changes from "Trial mode" to "Licensed for this system", quit LicenseControl.
  5. Relaunch Final Cut Pro. You're all done!


Unregister

If you have already registered PDF and you want to unregister it (for example, to move it to a different machine, or even sell your license) you can!

  1. Click on the Unregister button and the LicenseControl application will open. (This button is only visible if the plugin is already registered.)
  2. In LicenseControl, click on the Relinquish a License button. This will unregister the copy of PDF you registered previously, and allow you to register a different copy (perhaps on a different machine).


I upgraded PDF and now it says I'm unregistered. What's going on?

When upgrading PDF to a new version, you will need to re-register the plugin. (We're working on improving this behavior.) But don't worry: you don't need to dig out your serial number.

  1. Click the Enter Serial Number button. The LicenseControl application will open.
  2. Now click the Retrieve and Install an Existing License button in LicenseControl. This will register your upgraded version automatically!
